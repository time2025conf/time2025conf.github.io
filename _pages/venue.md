## QMUL

<p style="text-align: center"><img src="../assets/img/qb.jpg" width="75%"></p>

TIME 2025 will take place in the [https://www.qmul.ac.uk/about/howtofindus/mileend/index.html](Mile End campus) of Queen Mary University of London.

All the talks will take place in **Peston Lecture Theatre** at the New Graduate Centre - building n.18 in this [https://assets.qmul.ac.uk/docs/about/26065.pdf](campus map).

The closest tube stations are **Stepney Green** and **Mile End**. For travel planning to the venue we recommend using the "directions" option in this [https://maps.app.goo.gl/UdAfrhAvDUUkefqS6](google map). You can use the postal code E1 4DQ to locate the campus.
